Cookie Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026 · Effective from: 25 July 2026

We keep tracking to a minimum. AnotherWalk does not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies, and we do not follow you around the web.

1. What we use

To make the site work we use a small amount of local storage and a few strictly necessary cookies. We also measure visits with a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not use cookies.

WhatWhyType
Sign-in sessionKeeps you signed in after you use your magic linkNecessary (local storage)
Site protectionHelps keep the site secure and availableNecessary
Usage analyticsCounts visits so we can improve the routes — no cookies, no cross-site trackingMeasurement (cookieless)

2. Cookies set by others

When you pay, you are taken to Stripe's secure checkout. Stripe may set its own cookies there to process the payment and prevent fraud. Those cookies are governed by Stripe's own privacy and cookie notices.

3. Do we need your consent?

The storage we use is either strictly necessary to provide the service you asked for, or it is cookieless measurement that does not identify you. Under EU and Dutch rules, strictly necessary storage does not require consent. We will ask for your consent before placing any cookie that is not strictly necessary — and at the moment, we do not use any.

4. Managing storage in your browser

You can clear or block cookies and local storage in your browser settings. Note that if you block the necessary items, signing in and unlocking routes may stop working.

5. Changes

If we start using new cookies or tools, we will update this page and, where required, ask for your consent first.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@anotherwalk.city.
